Frequently Asked Questions

What is Plutus? ▼

Established in 2014, Plutus is on a mission to create a simple and honest platform that enables millions of cryptocurrency holders to maximize the potential of their assets. Plutus enables individuals to safely and securely buy, trade, and manage cryptocurrencies — all in one place. Our vision is an open, global financial system that is easily accessible to everyone.

Is the Plutus app currently accepting new users? ▼

The Plutus app is currently not accepting new users and is not available in the United States or to US persons. Existing users in supported regions can continue to access their accounts through the app.

Which cryptocurrencies does Plutus support? ▼

Over 75 cryptocurrencies are available to buy, sell, hold, or trade. You can exchange any supported currency to any other supported currency with no trading pair limitations, giving you the flexibility and freedom to manage your crypto your way.

Where is Plutus available? ▼

Plutus is available in select countries outside the United States. The app is not available to US persons. Some features may vary by region due to local regulations. Please refer to our terms of service for specific availability in your country.

What is my Recovery Phrase? ▼

Your recovery phrase is a set of words that allows you to restore access to your wallet if you lose your device or forget your PIN. It is extremely important that you write it down and store it in a safe place. Plutus Support will NEVER ask for your recovery phrase, 2FA code, or phone/email verification code.

How do I contact support? ▼

You can reach our support team at support@getplutus.com. Support hours are 6am–6pm PT (USA), Monday–Friday, excluding holidays. For additional FAQs, visit our help center.
